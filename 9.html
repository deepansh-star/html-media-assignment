HTML Text Formatting

  1. Bold(B)
    • Bold is semantically neutral, strong is preferred for importance.
    • It visually highlights important text within content.
    • HTML Bold tag is used for bold text formatting
  2. Strong
    • Emphasizes text with strong importance.
    • Renders text bold by default.
    • Shouldn't be used solely for styling; consider
  3. italic(i)
    • It's a non-semantic tag for applying italic style to text.
    • i doesn't convey meaning; it's often replaced by CSS styles.
    • For semantic meaning, use em for emphasized text instead.
  4. Underline (u)
    • Adds underlines to text content within the u element.
    • Considered a non-semantic tag; use CSS for styling instead.
    • Use sparingly, as underlining may be associated with hyperlinks.
  5. Emphasis
    • The em tag is used to emphasize text.
    • It renders text in italics for stylistic emphasis.
    • It conveys meaning, not just style, to browsers.
